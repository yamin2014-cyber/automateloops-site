Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Parently Loop ("App," "we," "us," "our") is designed for parents and guardians to organize and prioritize school-related emails. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Age Requirement: This App is intended for users 18 years of age or older. By using this App, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect data from users under 18.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Email Data

We collect emails exclusively from school-related sources, including:

  • Emails from your connected school domain(s) (e.g., school.edu, managebac.com)
  • Emails with subjects or content containing keywords such as "Classroom," "PTA," "School," "Teacher," or similar school-related terms

We do not access personal emails, financial or banking communications, medical or health information, or any emails unrelated to school.

2.2 Account Information

  • Your name and email address (from your sign-in provider: Apple, Google, or Microsoft)
  • Date of birth (to verify you are 18+)
  • School domain(s) you connect
  • Child information (names, grades, school associations) that you voluntarily add

2.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction logs (screens visited, features used)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Email Processing

Your school emails are processed by Manus AI to generate summaries of key information (dates, deadlines, action items), extract logistics tags (e.g., "PE kit," "permission slip"), and categorize emails by type (homework, payments, announcements).

Your email content is not stored permanently and is not used to train AI models. Processing is temporary and occurs only on-demand.

3.2 App Functionality

We use your data to display organized email summaries on your dashboard, create personalized action items and reminders, show child-specific information, and generate morning and evening briefings.

3.3 Legal & Safety

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect against fraud or security threats.

4. OAuth Scope & Email Access

4.1 Restricted Scope

We request read-only access to your email:

  • Gmail: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly
  • Outlook: Mail.Read

We cannot send emails, delete emails, or modify your mailbox.

4.2 Server-Side Filtering

Email filtering occurs before any data reaches our AI:

  1. Only emails from school domains or with school keywords are fetched
  2. Personal, financial, or sensitive emails are excluded
  3. You retain full control over which domains to connect

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Temporary Processing

  • Email summaries are cached locally on your device
  • Raw email content is not permanently stored on our servers
  • Cached data is deleted when you sign out or delete your account

5.2 Encryption

  • All data in transit uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Sensitive credentials (OAuth tokens) are stored securely on your device using platform keychain

5.3 Third-Party Processors

  • Manus AI: Processes email content for summarization (no permanent storage, no training use)
  • Apple / Google / Microsoft: Provide authentication and email access

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality.

Data is shared only with Manus AI (for email summarization, under strict data processing agreements), Apple / Google / Microsoft (for authentication and email access, as required by their platforms), and legal authorities if required by law.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

7.1 Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data at any time:

  1. Open Settings → Legal & Support → Data Deletion
  2. Confirm the action (this cannot be undone)
  3. All cached summaries are deleted and your OAuth token is revoked

7.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the App: Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account. This immediately revokes all OAuth tokens and wipes all data. You can also contact support@automateloops.com for assistance.

7.3 Data Access

To request a copy of your data or ask questions about what we store, email support@automateloops.com.

8. COPPA & Children's Privacy

This App is designed for parents and guardians (18+). While the App may reference children's information (names, grades), we do not collect data directly from children. All data is provided by the parent/guardian account holder.

We comply with COPPA (we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13) and FERPA (we respect school privacy regulations and only process school-related communications).

9. CCPA & California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete your personal information, and opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data). To exercise these rights, email support@automateloops.com.

10. International Data Transfers

If you access this App from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if required, by in-app notification.
