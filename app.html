Quick questions

Is it free?

Yes — connecting your school email and the Daily Loop are free to start. Premium adds more children and smarter alerts when you want them.

What exactly can it see?

Only emails from the school senders you choose, and only to read them. It cannot send, delete, or move anything. Your personal email stays personal.

When do I get access?

We're opening access to the waitlist in stages this school year — iPhone and Android. You'll get one email when it's your turn.
